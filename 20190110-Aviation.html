AVX project funded to refine Aviation Sustainment data

Jan 10, 2019

Data Refinement and Reduction for Aviation Sustainment

The team is tasked to develop a dashboard with rule-based relevancy engine to demonstrate the feasibility to refine the health/usage data. iDVL will support visualization using aviation sustainment data.

The work is in collaboration with:

  • AVX Aircraft Company, AVX was founded to bring advanced vertical takeoff and landing aircraft to the mass market.
  • Pennsylvania State University
  • People Tech

Students:

  • Ngan Nguyen, PhD student, Department of Computer Science, Texas Tech University
